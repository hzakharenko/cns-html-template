As the general election approaches on Nov. 8, voting resources are starting to appear as Maryland residents prepare to vote — but those resources might be in several different places that are hard to find. CNS’ 2022 General Election Guide is your tool to find those resources in one place, glimpse at what voting trends may look like and see how redistricting will affect this election.

Who is running for election?

The midterm elections in Maryland cover both national, state, and local seats. This season, one of the most anticipated elections is for Maryland governor, which is up for grabs after Republican Governor Larry Hogan sat for two-terms. The midterm elections also cover the seats in the U.S. House and U.S. Senate. With so many important races happening concurrently, it is helpful to know who is on the ballot before you get to the polls. This section will link you to the national and statewide candidates on the Maryland ballot, and supplies links to local offices as well.
